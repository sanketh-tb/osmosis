What's Osmosis?


Open Source Management of Organizational Synergy and Information Sharing

Hey there!

Osmosis is a simple tool that helps people discover new AI tools to help them in their workflow. These tools will get you upto speed and help you reduce time and effort giving you more bandwidth to focus on the important tasks - 10Xing your productivity.
Some examples on how AI can have an impact:

  • This website? AI made it. End to end.
  • Ditch Google. Use Perplexity. Better results with source citation.
  • Use cursor.sh instead of VSCode except when dealing with sensitive data.
  • To understand a PDF better, upload it to an LLM and chat with it.
  • Have to make a ppt in 10 mins? Use Canva's AI for generating slides.
  • So on and so forth. Start exploring some tools from the sidebar.

We are working on building propreitary closed loop AI tools for TB that'll accelerate our progress.
These tools will help everyone - from compliance to PMS and Engineering, save time and efficiently use the other tools at their disposal.
